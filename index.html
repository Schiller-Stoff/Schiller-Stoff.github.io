Book

Erinnerungskultur und Holocaust Education im digitalen Wandel: Georeferenzierte Dokumentations-, Erinnerungs- und Vermittlungsprojekte

2024 Collected edition

This collection examines how remembrance of National Socialism and the Holocaust is evolving alongside digital technologies. Contributors analyze the growing importance of digital Holocaust education, particularly through geo-referenced web applications that mark memorial sites on digital maps and enhance them with additional context through Deep Mapping techniques. The work presents critical perspectives on various digital documentation, memorial, and educational projects, exploring how these technological approaches are reshaping our engagement with historical memory.
